# Transformation-of-Fast-Retailing-s-business-post-COVID-19-world
Tableau Data Visualisation and Business Recommendation for Fast Retailing’s business post COVID-19 world in a growing e-commerce business presence
Business problem
The COVID-19 has greatly pressured the survival of many companies and businesses such as the apparel retailers. 
Some of the problems faced may include cashflows problem, impacted sales, zero revenue, closed stores due to forced governmental policies or closed borders, 
and high rising cost eg. courier charges, rental charges, manpower costs to maintain the overall operations of the business. Due to all the earlier mentioned reasons, 
the uncertainties of COVID-19 have greatly impacted the business survival and business profitability. It has also presented a challenging business survival and profitability issues; 
therefore, fast-changing tactics are required to be adopted for Fast Retailing group eg. stronger e-commerce technology platform.

Recommendations
Our recommendations for the post COVID-19 include accelerate Ariake project for e-commerce success, expand in growing markets such as Greater China, 
Vietnam, and Singapore. We continue to monitor good financial soundness to maintain profitability.
